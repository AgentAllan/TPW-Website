The Journey Begins

Long ago, there were two gods in existence. The Dragon God and the Phoenix God. They lived in a void of darkness until they both used their powers to create the universe. The Dragon God created the monsters and creatures that roamed the earth. To balance the universe, the Phoenix God created humanity, who were born with natural talent and potential. This not only upset the Dragon God, as humanity proved to be superior to his creations, but he also feared them, and their power. So, he attempted to eradicate them. But, the Phoenix God stepped in and they engaged in eternal combat, eventually leading to humanity's strongest warrior to seal the two gods within special crystals guarded by two royal kingdoms. Now, someone seeks to awaken the Dragon God. Can you stop the universe form being overcome with darkness?
